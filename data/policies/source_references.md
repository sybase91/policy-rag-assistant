# Source References and Data Disclaimer

The Acme Corp policy documents in this repository are **fictional** and created for demonstration purposes only.

They are inspired by common structures found in publicly available policy examples, such as:

- Public university travel and expense policies
- Public nonprofit employee handbook examples
- Public gifts and hospitality policies
- Public anti-corruption and compliance guidance
- Public HR policy templates

**No real company confidential policy text is included.**

The purpose of these documents is to provide a realistic mock corpus for testing a RAG-based policy assistant and the PolicyOps Agent workflow.

**Important:** These policies are not legal, HR, finance, or compliance advice. Do not use them for real workplace decisions.
