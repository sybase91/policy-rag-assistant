# Acme Corp Travel and Expense Policy

Policy Owner: Finance Team  
Effective Date: 2026-01-01  
Applies To: All Acme Corp employees and contractors

## TE-001 Purpose

This policy defines which travel and business expenses may be reimbursed by Acme Corp and how employees should plan, document, and submit those expenses.

## TE-002 General Reimbursement Rule

Employees may request reimbursement for reasonable, necessary, and pre-approved business expenses incurred while performing Acme Corp duties.

## TE-003 Business Travel

Business travel must be booked through approved channels when possible. Employees should choose economical options and document the business purpose of the trip.

## TE-004 Client Meals

Client meals may be reimbursed when there is a clear business purpose, named external attendees, an itemized receipt, and manager approval.

## TE-005 Client Meal Thresholds

Client meals above INR 10,000 require manager approval. Client meals above INR 25,000 require Finance approval before the event when possible.

## TE-006 Alcohol

Alcohol is not reimbursable unless explicitly approved by Finance before the event.

## TE-007 Personal Card Usage

Employees may pay with a personal card and request reimbursement, provided all required documentation is submitted within the reimbursement timeline.

## TE-008 Required Documentation

Employees must submit an itemized receipt, business purpose, attendee names, date, location, and approval record for meal and travel claims.

## TE-009 Non-Reimbursable Expenses

Personal entertainment, fines, traffic violations, and expenses without a clear business purpose are not reimbursable.

## TE-010 Hotel Upgrades

Hotel room upgrades are not reimbursable unless required for accessibility, safety, or explicitly pre-approved by a manager for a documented business reason.

## TE-011 Escalation

If an employee is unsure whether an expense is reimbursable, they must contact Finance before submitting the claim.

### Example

A client dinner for INR 18,000 with two external guests may be eligible if there is a documented business purpose, itemized receipt, guest names, and required approvals based on the amount thresholds.
