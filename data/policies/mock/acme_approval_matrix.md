# Acme Corp Approval Matrix

Policy Owner: Finance and People Operations  
Effective Date: 2026-01-01  
Applies To: All Acme Corp employees and managers

## AM-001 Purpose

This matrix defines who must approve common policy-related requests at Acme Corp.

## AM-002 Manager Approval

Managers approve routine travel, local expenses, client meals up to INR 10,000, and short remote work arrangements.

## AM-003 Finance Approval

Finance approves client meals above INR 10,000, reimbursement exceptions, lost-receipt claims above INR 2,000, and non-standard travel expenses.

## AM-004 HR Approval

HR approves extended remote work, medical-related workplace accommodations, and cross-border work requests.

## AM-005 Legal Approval

Legal approves gifts or hospitality involving public officials, unusual vendor benefits, and high-risk third-party engagements.

## AM-006 Information Security Approval

Information Security approves external sharing of confidential or customer data, vendor access to production systems, and exceptions to data handling rules.

## AM-007 Approval Thresholds Summary

| Request type | Approver |
|--------------|----------|
| Client meal up to INR 10,000 | Manager |
| Client meal above INR 10,000 | Manager + Finance |
| Client meal above INR 25,000 | Manager + Finance (pre-approval preferred) |
| Vendor gift above INR 10,000 | Manager + Compliance |
| External customer data sharing | Manager + Information Security |
| Remote work over 5 days | Manager + HR |

## AM-008 Escalation

If the required approver is unavailable, employees must not assume approval and should contact the policy owner team listed in the relevant policy.
