# Acme Corp Data Access Policy

Policy Owner: Information Security  
Effective Date: 2026-01-01  
Applies To: All Acme Corp employees and contractors

## DA-001 Purpose

This policy defines how employees access, use, and share Acme Corp information, including customer, HR, and finance data.

## DA-002 Need-to-Know Access

Employees may access only the data required for their role and approved business purpose.

## DA-003 Confidential Documents

Confidential documents must not be shared externally without documented approval and appropriate safeguards.

## DA-004 Customer Data

Customer data may not be shared with external vendors unless a business need is documented, a data processing agreement is in place, and Information Security approval is obtained.

## DA-005 HR Data

HR data is restricted to HR and authorized managers. Employees may access only their own records unless explicitly authorized.

## DA-006 Finance Data

Finance data may be shared only with employees who require it for accounting, audit, or approved business operations.

## DA-007 External Sharing Controls

Before sharing data with an external party, employees must confirm classification, approved transfer method, and required approvals.

## DA-008 Security Escalation

Suspected unauthorized access or data leakage must be reported to Information Security immediately.

## DA-009 Prohibited Actions

Employees may not upload customer or confidential data to personal email, personal cloud storage, or unapproved AI tools.

## DA-010 Escalation

Any request to share customer data with an external vendor for analysis requires Information Security review at minimum.

### Example

Sharing customer data with an external vendor for analysis is not allowed without documented business need, contractual controls, and Information Security approval.
