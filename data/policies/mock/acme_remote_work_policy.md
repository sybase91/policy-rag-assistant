# Acme Corp Remote Work Policy

Policy Owner: People Operations  
Effective Date: 2026-01-01  
Applies To: All Acme Corp employees

## RW-001 Purpose

This policy defines when employees may work remotely and what approvals are required.

## RW-002 Standard Remote Work

Occasional work-from-home for convenience requires manager awareness but not formal approval for single days.

## RW-003 Extended Remote Work

Remote work for more than 5 consecutive business days requires manager approval and a documented plan for availability and deliverables.

## RW-004 Medical Exceptions

Medical-related remote work requests should be supported by appropriate documentation when requested for more than 5 consecutive business days and routed to HR for review.

## RW-005 Cross-Border Remote Work

Working remotely from outside the employee's assigned country requires HR and Legal approval in advance.

## RW-006 Data Security

Employees working remotely must use approved devices, secure networks, and follow Acme Corp data access rules.

## RW-007 Equipment Reimbursement

Standard remote work does not include reimbursement for personal furniture or internet unless covered by a separate stipend program.

## RW-008 Attendance Expectations

Employees must remain reachable during core business hours unless an alternate schedule is approved by their manager.

## RW-009 Escalation

Disputes about remote work eligibility should be escalated to HR and the employee's management chain.

### Example

Working from home for two weeks due to a medical reason may be allowed with manager and HR review depending on role requirements and documentation.
