# Acme Corp Gifts and Hospitality Policy

Policy Owner: Legal and Compliance Team  
Effective Date: 2026-01-01  
Applies To: All Acme Corp employees and contractors

## GH-001 Purpose

This policy governs giving and receiving gifts, hospitality, and business courtesies to prevent conflicts of interest and compliance risk.

## GH-002 Accepting Gifts

Employees may accept modest business courtesies that are infrequent, transparent, and cannot reasonably influence business decisions.

## GH-003 Gift Value Thresholds

Gifts from vendors or clients above INR 5,000 must be reported to Compliance. Gifts above INR 10,000 require manager and Compliance approval before acceptance.

## GH-004 Cash and Cash Equivalents

Cash, gift cards, and cash-equivalent rewards are never acceptable from vendors, clients, or partners unless explicitly approved under a formal program.

## GH-005 Giving Gifts to Clients

Employees giving gifts to clients must keep values reasonable, document the business purpose, and obtain manager approval for gifts above INR 5,000.

## GH-006 Gift Register

Gifts above INR 5,000 must be recorded in the Acme Corp gift register within 5 business days.

## GH-007 Public Officials

Gifts or hospitality involving public officials require Legal approval before offer or acceptance.

## GH-008 Conflicts of Interest

Employees must decline gifts that create or appear to create a conflict of interest.

## GH-009 Escalation

Compliance must review any gift or hospitality request involving unusual value, repeated offers, or sensitive counterparties.

### Example

A vendor gift valued at INR 12,000 requires reporting and approval before acceptance under this policy.
