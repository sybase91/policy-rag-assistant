# Acme Corp Reimbursement Policy

Policy Owner: Finance Team  
Effective Date: 2026-01-01  
Applies To: All Acme Corp employees and contractors

## RE-001 Purpose

This policy explains how employees submit reimbursement requests and what documentation Finance requires.

## RE-002 Submission Timeline

Employees must submit reimbursement requests within 30 days of the expense date unless a documented exception is approved.

## RE-003 Required Documentation

Each reimbursement request must include date, amount, currency, business purpose, payment method, and supporting receipts where applicable.

## RE-004 Missing Receipts

If a receipt is lost, the employee must submit a lost-receipt declaration, proof of payment when available, and manager approval. Finance may approve partial reimbursement at its discretion.

## RE-005 Personal Payment Methods

Personal card, UPI, and cash payments may be reimbursed when supported by valid documentation and policy-compliant business purpose.

## RE-006 Expense Categories

Eligible categories include travel, meals with clients, local transport for business purposes, and approved supplies. Personal purchases are not eligible.

## RE-007 Duplicate Claims

Employees may not submit duplicate claims for the same expense. Duplicate submissions may be rejected and escalated to Finance.

## RE-008 Currency Handling

Expenses should be submitted in the currency paid. Foreign currency expenses must include the exchange rate or card statement detail used for conversion.

## RE-009 Late Submissions

Late submissions beyond 30 days require manager approval and may be denied if documentation is incomplete.

## RE-010 Escalation

Unresolved reimbursement questions should be escalated to the Finance helpdesk before payment is expected.
